The problem

European VC recruiting is a black box

✕

Everything you know about VC is scattered across 50 newsletters, 30 podcasts, and random Twitter threads. There's no single source that explains how European VC recruiting actually works.

✕

Nobody tells you what funds are actually looking for. It's not the same as IB or PE — the criteria aren't written down anywhere, and every fund evaluates differently.

✕

There's no standardized process. No on-cycle. No headhunters. No Glassdoor threads with "here's what they asked me." You're navigating blind.

✕
