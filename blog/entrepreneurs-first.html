Why Entrepreneurs First: Programs, Careers, & Portfolio

Last updated April 2025

Overview

Entrepreneurs First pioneered the idea of talent investing — backing exceptional individuals before they have a cofounder, a team, or even an idea. Founded in 2011 by Matt Clifford and Alice Bentinck, both former McKinsey consultants, EF has helped create 600+ companies collectively valued at over $16B. The firm raised $158M in 2022 and has expanded from London to Bangalore, Paris, Toronto, and San Francisco.

EF's model is fundamentally different from traditional VC. Rather than evaluating companies, EF evaluates people. The firm runs structured cohort programs where selected individuals spend 12 weeks finding a cofounder and developing an idea. The strongest teams pass Investment Committee, receive a $250K check, and enter a further 12-week build phase. In 2023 EF shifted its centre of gravity to San Francisco, requiring all companies to relocate to the Bay Area and incorporate in the US before Demo Day.

Programs

Core Program — Runs twice a year across London, Paris, and Bangalore. You join as an individual, pre-idea and pre-team. Over 12 weeks you find a cofounder and develop a company. The strongest teams receive $250K ($125K for 8% via a SAFE plus an optional $125K MFN SAFE) and relocate to San Francisco for a further 12 weeks before Demo Day.

The Bridge — Launched in 2025. An eight-week residency in San Francisco for European founders at the very earliest stage. Cohorts of 40-50 people live together in a hacker house. Housing, workspace, and food are covered in full. At the end, teams that are ready pitch to EF's Investment Committee. The strongest receive $250K and stay in San Francisco for a further three months.

Careers at EF

EF hires Talent Investors — not a traditional VC associate role. Talent Investors find exceptional individuals, run hackathons and campus events, advise founders on the programme, and help them raise at Demo Day. The role gives exposure to hundreds of founders per year and is a credible launchpad into VC or founding your own company. Previous Talent Investors have gone on to join Sequoia, Balderton, and Accel, or have become founders themselves. Roles are posted at joinef.com/about/careers.
