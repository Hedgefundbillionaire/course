Why Eurazeo Venture: Interviews, Careers, & Portfolio

Last updated April 2025

Overview

Eurazeo is one of Europe's largest and most established private markets firms, listed on Euronext Paris. The group manages €36B in total assets across private equity, private debt, and real assets. Its venture and growth arm — which is what this page covers — manages €4B+ across two distinct strategies: Venture Capital (seed to Series B) and Growth (Series B to Series C).

The venture team has been backing European tech founders for over 20 years, with a particular focus on digital health, fintech, smart cities, and enterprise software. The firm operates from Paris, London, and Berlin, giving it genuine pan-European reach. The growth team, led by Hala Fadel, invests in later-stage companies across a broader set of sectors.

What makes Eurazeo different from most European VCs is the institutional backing. As a publicly listed company, Eurazeo has permanent capital and a longer investment horizon than typical fund structures allow. This means the firm can be patient with portfolio companies in a way that funds with fixed lifespans cannot. It also means Eurazeo can invest from its own balance sheet alongside its fund vehicles.

Interview Process at Eurazeo

Eurazeo runs a structured process that skews toward candidates with finance backgrounds — ex-investment bankers and strategy consultants are common hires. The process combines fit and case study components.

Fit interviews cover your background, why you want to work in venture and growth investing, and your views on the European tech market. Expect questions about your deal experience, your investment framework, and why Eurazeo specifically.

Case study — Eurazeo sends you a selection of companies and asks you to analyze them and say whether you would invest or not. Write up your investment view on each. The emphasis is on the quality of your reasoning and your ability to form a clear, defensible opinion — not the depth of financial modelling.

Case review — you present and defend your written work in a live conversation with a senior team member who will challenge your conclusions. They want to see how you handle pushback and whether you can update your thinking when presented with new information.

Careers at Eurazeo Venture

Eurazeo is one of the larger VC employers in Europe, with a 15-person growth team across Paris, London, and Berlin. The venture and growth teams are distinct — candidates should target whichever matches their background and stage preference. The growth team has hired ex-bankers and strategy consultants. The venture team skews toward candidates with more operational or technical backgrounds.

The firm hires at analyst and associate level on a more regular basis than most boutique seed funds. Roles are posted on the Eurazeo website. Given the firm's focus on digital health, fintech, and smart cities, candidates with domain expertise in those sectors have a meaningful advantage over generalists.
