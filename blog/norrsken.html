Norrsken VC Overview

Norrsken VC is Europe's leading impact venture fund. Founded by Niklas Adalberth, co-founder of Klarna. Fund II closed in 2024 at €320M — the largest early-stage generalist impact fund in Europe. SFDR Article 9 compliant: every investment must align with at least one UN Sustainable Development Goal. The firm targets market-rate returns, not concessionary impact.

Norrsken VC AUM

€320M (Fund II, 2024). Portfolio spans climate tech, energy, healthtech, biotech, and education. 2 unicorns: Einride and 1KOMMA5°.
