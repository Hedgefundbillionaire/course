Why Phoenix Court / LocalGlobe: Interviews, Careers, & Portfolio

Last updated April 2025

Overview

Phoenix Court Group is the parent organisation of LocalGlobe, Latitude, Solar, and Basecamp — four funds covering every stage from pre-seed to the public markets. Founded by Robin and Saul Klein, the group rebranded from LocalGlobe to Phoenix Court in 2022 after raising $500M. LocalGlobe remains the core fund at pre-seed and seed. Latitude backs Series B and C. Solar focuses on pre-IPO. Basecamp backs emerging seed managers.

The Klein family brings an unusual combination of operator experience and multi-generational investing to the firm. Robin Klein was an early investor in Skype and LastMinute.com. Saul Klein co-founded LoveFilm (acquired by Amazon) and served as VP of Marketing at Skype. This operator DNA means the team evaluates founders differently from traditional finance-background VCs — they know what it feels like to build and scale a company.

The firm also puts its money where its values are. Phoenix Court donates 10% of profits and 2% of carry to Phoenix Court Works, a foundation supporting the Somers Town neighbourhood in North London. For candidates who care about impact alongside returns, this is a genuine differentiator.

Interview Process at Phoenix Court / LocalGlobe

LocalGlobe's process combines fit conversations, market sizing, and investment case work. Expect to discuss which companies and markets excite you, walk through your past experience, and defend an investment thesis under pressure. The team values candidates who understand the European seed ecosystem — not just headline companies, but the deeper layers of the market where LocalGlobe operates.

Careers at Phoenix Court

Phoenix Court recruits selectively with no formal on-cycle process. The firm values candidates who genuinely understand what makes great founders and can form conviction before the data confirms it. Most hires come through direct network or referral. Attending LocalGlobe events and engaging with the broader London seed ecosystem is the most credible path to a conversation.
