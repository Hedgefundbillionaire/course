Why Sequoia Capital: Interviews, Careers, & Portfolio

Last updated April 2025

Overview

Sequoia Capital is the most storied venture capital firm in history. Don Valentine founded it in 1972 with Atari as its first investment. The portfolio since then reads like a history of technology itself: Apple, Cisco, Oracle, Google, YouTube, WhatsApp, Airbnb, and Stripe. Sequoia became fully independent from its Asia affiliates in 2023 — the US/Europe, India/Southeast Asia, and China teams now operate as separate firms under different names.

In Europe, Sequoia established a dedicated team in 2022 led by Luciana Lixandru, formerly a partner at Accel. The European team focuses on seed through Series B investments and has already backed companies like Mistral AI (Paris) and Wayve (London). The office is in London but the team is active across the continent, with particular strength in AI, deeptech, and enterprise software.

The Sequoia brand carries unmatched weight with founders. For candidates, working at or being trained by Sequoia signals excellence in a way that few other firms can match. The firm's internal culture emphasizes deep company research, long-term partnership with founders, and rigorous intellectual honesty.

Interview Process at Sequoia

Sequoia's European team is small and does not run formal on-cycle recruiting. Most hires come through direct network or referral. When the team does hire, the process involves deep investment thesis discussions, company evaluations, and partner interviews that test your ability to identify and articulate what makes a company exceptional rather than merely good.

The US team recruits more formally with analyst and associate roles. The bar is extraordinarily high — Sequoia receives more applications per role than almost any other VC firm globally.

Careers at Sequoia

For the European team specifically, candidates with operator backgrounds at top European tech companies are valued alongside traditional finance profiles. The team is building from the ground up and values people who can source deals, evaluate founders, and support portfolio companies hands-on. If you are targeting Sequoia Europe, the most credible approach is direct outreach to Luciana Lixandru or Julien Bek with a specific, thoughtful view on a European market or company.
