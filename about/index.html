Our mission

We built what we wished existed when we were recruiting

In 2025, we created Buyside School with one simple mission: help the next generation of investors. Having gone through multiple stages of recruiting processes ourselves, we know how stressful the process can be. We're here to share with you everything we learned.

We are a small team of former professionals from the world's top Venture Firms. We've not only went through the recruiting process that you're currently undergoing, but have also sat on the opposite side of the table evaluating candidates.
